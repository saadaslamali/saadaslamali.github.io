KAYAL
MARCH 2023
This is a collaborative project with Kayal, a sustainable fashion brand from Pakistan that emphasizes a connection to nature in their designs.
Using animations made with Stable Diffusion and TouchDesigner , I was able to bring their block-print designs to life. The animations featured nature-inspired elements like flowers, birds, and butterflies, aligning with the brand's aesthetic.
The goal was to merge traditional block print designs with technology by infusing AI-driven animations into Kayal's artwork. This project celebrated the fusion of heritage artistry with modern digital innovation.
