"Some Thoughts I Had" is a self-published, augmented reality art book. It is my attempt to visualize & give form to thoughts and feelings that I am otherwise unable to describe.
The artwork consists of sketches from my personal diary. Each page is accompanied by a seamlessly looping animation made using a combination of recorded videos, hand-drawn animations and TouchDesigner visuals. These animations can be accessed through using an augmented-reality app made in Unity.
The purpose of the animations is to serve as a visual representation of the different layers of thoughts, mirroring the abstract, ephemeral and often chaotic nature of human introspection. Through the use of augmented-reality, my aim is to create a more dynamic and immersive viewing experience.
12 PAGES, 8.5"x5.5"
