SOME THOUGHTS I HAD

Overview

JANUARY 2023
Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects, TouchDesigner, C++
"Some Thoughts I Had" is a self-published, augmented reality art book. It is my attempt to visualize & give form to thoughts and feelings that I am otherwise unable to describe.
The purpose of the animations is to serve as a visual representation of the different layers of thought, mirroring the abstract and often chaotic nature of human introspection.
Through the use of augmented-reality, my aim is to create a dynamic and immersive viewing experience that challenges preconcieved notions of what a book can be.

Process

The artwork consists of sketches, drawn in ink, from my personal diary. Each page is accompanied by a seamlessly looping animation made using a combination of recorded videos, hand-drawn animations and TouchDesigner visuals. These animations can be accessed through using an augmented-reality app made in Unity using C++.
