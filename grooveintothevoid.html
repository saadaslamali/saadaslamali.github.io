GROOVE INTO THE VOID

Overview

MAY 2023 - PRESENT
Hydra.js, JavaScript, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop
"Groove Into The Void" is a community-based music event and cultural exchange based in Karachi, Pakistan. It seeks to curate audio-visual experiences and foster a subculture for music and art enthusiasts in a city with a scarcity of safe-spaces for artistic expression.
Through our ongoing collaboration, I have played a multifaceted role that extends from live-coded visual performances to the creation of motion graphics, animated posters, and promotional videos that embody the brand's DIY, experimental, and vibrant visual identity
This collaboration has deepened my understanding of how people connect with artwork and the dynamics of community interaction. It's a platform for channeling my passion for creative technology to foster connections grounded in community values, enhancing both artistic expressions and the collective experience.

Live-Coded Visuals

In May, 2023, I took on the role of live-coding audio-reactive visuals using JavaScript and Hydra.js for the "Controlled Chaos" event. The 8-hour long improvised performance allowed real-time engagement with both the audience and music performers, resulting in an immersive and interactive experience.
Below are some examples of visuals that were created during the event. These visuals, originally audio-reactive, have been converted to a web friendly version that responds to the position of your cursor.
const { voronoi, noise, osc, shape, o0, mouse, src, width, height } = synth osc(2,0.5,12) .modulateKaleid(osc(5,0.4,()=>mouse.x*5/width),()=>mouse.y*20/height) .colorama(-0.6) .luma(0.2) .rotate(3,0.3) .saturate(4) .modulatePixelate(o0,()=>mouse.y*50/height,()=>mouse.x*-50/width+10) .brightness(-0.1) .modulateScale(o0,()=>mouse.y*-1/height+0.5) .contrast(1.7) .saturate(2) .blend(o0,0.7) .scale(()=>mouse.y*0.01/height+1,()=>mouse.x*0.05/width+1) .out();
const { voronoi, osc, noise, shape, o0, mouse, src, width, height } = synth shape(99,()=>mouse.x*0.25/width+0.1,0.06) //.modulateRepeat(o0,99) .rotate(3,-0.8) .modulatePixelate(noise(4,()=>mouse.y*0.1/height),()=>mouse.y*50/height) .mult(osc(12,0.4,0.1).colorama(-99).kaleid(99).modulatePixelate(o0,99,99).modulateScale(o0,()=>mouse.y*-1/height)) .brightness(0.4) .saturate(7) .modulateScale(src(o0).modulate(noise(2,0.3)),()=>mouse.x/width) .contrast(12) .blend(o0,0.975) .scale(1.01,1.01) .out();
const { voronoi, osc, noise, shape, o0, mouse, src, width, height } = synth osc(80, -0.1, 0.3) .diff(osc(10,0.01, 0.01)) .modulateRepeat( noise(()=>mouse.y/height, 0.25).modulateScale( osc(15).rotate(()=>mouse.y*2/height,0.5) ), 0.6 ) .color(7, 2, 2) .contrast(1) .diff(src(o0).modulate(o0, 0.04), 0.8) .modulateScrollY(o0,()=>mouse.y*10/height,0) .invert() .modulateScale(osc(2),()=>mouse.x*-2/width+1) .rotate(0,0.1) .contrast(1.2) .saturate(1.1) .blend(o0,0.8) .scale(()=>mouse.x*-0.01/width+1,()=>mouse.x*0.01/width+1) .out()
